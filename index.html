Description

A delicious vegetarian lasagna filled with fresh vegetables and creamy ricotta cheese.

Ingredients

  • 4 Organic Tomatoes
  • 2 cups Whole Wheat Flour
  • 200g Fresh Mozzarella
  • 1 cup Ricotta Cheese
  • 2 cloves Garlic

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)
  2. Prepare tomato sauce by simmering tomatoes and garlic
  3. Layer lasagna noodles, sauce, and cheeses in baking dish
  4. Bake for 45 minutes until golden and bubbly
  5. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving
